The Sequence

14 Phases.
Executed in Order.

Sequence is everything in post-civil work. Each phase below must be fully complete before the next begins — this is how we prevent rework and cost overruns.

01
Site Assessment & Planning
Full walkthrough, room-by-room measuring, structural condition report, scope finalization, and itemized quotation issued within 48 hours.
Pre-Work
02
Waterproofing
Terrace, all bathrooms, balconies, and kitchen area — liquid waterproofing membrane applied and tested with water ponding. Done before any flooring or tiling begins.
Day 1 Priority
03
Plumbing (Concealed)
All concealed water supply lines and drain pipes chased into walls and slabs. Pressure-tested before walls are closed. Includes hot and cold lines, drain slopes.
04
Electrical (Concealed)
Conduit laying, wiring, switch box and MCC box fixing, earthing — all concealed inside walls. Inspected and signed off before plastering begins.
05
Plastering & Wall Finishing
Internal wall plaster to smooth finish. Patch repairs around all conduit and pipe chases. Ready for paint and tile.
06
False Ceiling
Gypsum board or grid ceiling system installed after plumbing and electrical are fully concealed. Lighting tray layouts, cove profiles, and AC duct openings fixed at this stage.
07
Flooring & Tiling
Vitrified tiles, marble, granite, or hardwood as per approved design. Bathroom wall tiles. Threshold strips and skirting. Kitchen backsplash.
08
Doors & Windows — Interior Finishing
Door frame fitting, door shutters, all hardware (handles, hinges, locks, closers). Window beading, sealing, mosquito mesh channels. All interior woodwork around openings.
Core Expertise
09
Carpentry & Interior Woodwork
Modular kitchen carcass and shutters, wardrobes, TV units, study tables, shoe racks, bathroom vanities — all designed and built to specification by our in-house carpentry team.
Core Expertise
10
Painting
Primer + putty + 2 coats of approved wall paint. Ceiling white. Wood polish or enamel on all carpentry. Texture finish if specified. No painting before carpentry is complete.
11
Electrical Fittings (Open)
Switches, sockets, MCB wiring, lights, fans, AC points, and all open electrical fittings — installed only after painting is 100% complete.
12
Plumbing Fittings (Open)
Sanitary ware, taps, showers, geyser connections, kitchen sink — installed only after tiling and painting are complete. No fittings before paint.
13
Furniture Delivery & Installation
Readymade or custom furniture delivered and placed as per client-approved layout. Assembled, positioned, and styled by our team. All in one delivery window.
New Service
14
Snag & Handover
Full walkthrough with client. Every punch-list item documented and resolved. Final deep clean. Keys handed over. 12-month workmanship warranty begins.

Your Role

What You
Need to Do

We handle the execution. Here is what we need from you to keep the project on time.

Before Work Starts
  • Civil structure fully complete — RCC, brick, external plaster, terrace slab
  • Site access confirmed at agreed working hours (8 AM – 6 PM)
  • Construction debris cleared from inside the flat
  • Final floor plan confirmed — no structural changes after this
  • Design preferences signed off — tiles, paint, wood finishes
During Execution
  • Respond to material approval requests within 24 hours
  • Payments made at each milestone on time
  • Client-supplied materials delivered to site before that phase starts
  • One point of contact from client side for all decisions
For Furniture Service
  • Design references submitted at least 2 weeks before furniture phase
  • Furniture layout and item list approved before orders are placed
  • Site clean and paint fully dry before delivery date
  • Client or representative present during delivery and placement
